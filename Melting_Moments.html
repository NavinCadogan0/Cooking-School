Melting Moments Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 180g butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup icing sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1/3 cup cornflour

    • Buttercream Filling:

  • 60g butter, softened
  • 2/3 cup icing sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 160Â°C. Line baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Beat butter, icing sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy.
  3. Sift flour and cornflour together, then gradually stir into butter mixture.
  4. Roll teaspoons of mixture into small balls.
  5. Place on prepared trays and flatten slightly with a fork.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until light golden.
  7. Cool completely on wire racks.
  8. For filling, beat butter until creamy. Gradually beat in icing sugar and vanilla.
  9. Join pairs of biscuits with buttercream filling.
