Jam Drops Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 125g butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/4 cup raspberry jam

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Line baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Cream butter, sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy.
  3. Add egg and beat well.
  4. Gradually stir in flour until a soft dough forms.
  5. Roll tablespoons of mixture into balls.
  6. Place on prepared trays, allowing room for spreading.
  7. Make an indentation in the center of each ball using your thumb.
  8. Fill each indentation with approximately 1/2 teaspoon of raspberry jam.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until lightly golden.
  10. Cool on trays for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
