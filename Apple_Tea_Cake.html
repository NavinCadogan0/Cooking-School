Apple Tea Cake

Ingredients:

  • 125g butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup caster sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon caster sugar, extra
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease and line a 20cm round cake pan.
  2. Using an electric mixer, cream butter, sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Fold in flour and milk alternately, starting and ending with flour.
  5. Pour mixture into prepared pan and smooth the surface.
  6. Arrange apple slices in a circular pattern on top of the cake batter.
  7. Mix extra sugar and cinnamon together and sprinkle over the apples.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  10. Turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Serve warm or at room temperature, optionally dusted with icing sugar.
