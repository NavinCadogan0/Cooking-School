Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour two 23cm round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry ingredients.
  4. Mix with an electric mixer on medium speed for about 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in hot water. The batter will be very thin.
  6. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  8. Cool in pans for 10 minutes.
  9. Remove from pans and cool completely on wire racks.
  10. Once cooled, frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.
