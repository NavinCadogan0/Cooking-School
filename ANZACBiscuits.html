ANZAC Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup desiccated coconut
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 125g butter
  • 2 tablespoons golden syrup
  • 1 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 tablespoons boiling water

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Line baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Combine flour, oats, coconut and brown sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Melt butter and golden syrup in a small saucepan over low heat.
  4. Mix bicarbonate of soda with boiling water in a small bowl, then add to butter mixture.
  5. Pour butter mixture into dry ingredients and mix well.
  6. Roll level tablespoons of mixture into balls and place on trays, allowing room for spreading.
  7. Press down slightly with a fork.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Allow to cool on trays for 5 minutes before transferring to wire racks.
