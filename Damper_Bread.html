Damper Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 80g butter, chilled and cubed
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water (approximately)

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Sift flour and salt into a large bowl.
  3. Using your fingertips, rub butter into flour mixture until it resembles fine breadcrumbs.
  4. Make a well in the center. Add milk and enough water to make a soft, sticky dough.
  5. Turn onto a lightly floured surface. Knead gently until smooth (don't over-knead).
  6. Shape into a 20cm round loaf.
  7. Place on prepared tray. Score top with a sharp knife to make a cross pattern.
  8. Dust with extra flour.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown and hollow sounding when tapped.
  10. Serve warm with butter.
