Blueberry Scones

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 175g cold butter, cubed
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Extra cream and coarse sugar for topping

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gently fold in blueberries.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together cream, eggs, and vanilla.
  6. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  7. Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead a few times.
  8. Pat into a circle about 2.5cm thick.
  9. Cut into 8 wedges and place on prepared baking tray.
  10. Brush tops with cream and sprinkle with coarse sugar.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  12. Cool on wire rack for 10 minutes before serving.
  13. Best served warm with butter or clotted cream.
