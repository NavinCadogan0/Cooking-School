Plain Sponge Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup caster sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup plain flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 60g butter, melted and cooled

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease and line two 20cm round cake pans with baking paper.
  2. Using an electric mixer, beat eggs, sugar and vanilla on high speed for 8-10 minutes until thick, pale and tripled in volume.
  3. Sift flour, cornflour, baking powder and salt together three times.
  4. Gently fold dry ingredients into egg mixture in three batches, using a large metal spoon.
  5. Pour melted butter down the side of the bowl and gently fold through until just combined.
  6. Divide mixture evenly between prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until cakes spring back when lightly touched.
  8. Allow to cool in pans for 5 minutes.
  9. Turn onto wire racks lined with baking paper to cool completely.
  10. Once cooled, sandwich layers together with whipped cream and dust top with icing sugar if desired.
